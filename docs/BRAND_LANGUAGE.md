# The Funni Farm Brand Language

## Positioning

The Funni Farm is a small-batch hemp and CBG wellness brand. Products should be
described as non-intoxicating, CBG-rich, farm-crafted, COA-transparent,
and intended for adults.

Core message:

- CBG-rich adult wellness.
- Non-intoxicating hemp products.
- Small-batch hemp wellness.
- Farm-crafted hemp wellness.
- Farm-made, COA-transparent products.
- Simple plant-based additions to adult daily routines.

## Approved Phrases

- Non-intoxicating hemp wellness.
- CBG-rich hemp products.
- CBG-rich where supported by the product label and COA.
- Farm-crafted hemp wellness.
- CBG-rich daily wellness.
- Made for adult wellness routines.
- Small-batch CBG products.
- Plant-based daily ritual.
- Farm-crafted hemp goods.
- COA-transparent where current batch records are available.
- Batch-labeled for transparency.
- Made with care, clarity, and good ingredients.
- Simple hemp support for everyday life.
- Adult-use hemp wellness product.
- Review the product label, ingredients, and COA before use.

## Banned Phrases

Do not use these in public-facing marketing, product descriptions, metadata,
ads, reviews, or calls to action:

- Stoner.
- Weed.
- Getting high.
- Legal high.
- Psychoactive.
- Delta-8.
- Delta 8.
- D8.
- THCA.
- THC products.
- Recreational.
- Cannabis buzz.
- Euphoric high.
- Mind-altering.
- Smoke shop.
- Dispensary-style language.
- Party language.
- Candy-for-kids energy.
- Old cannabinoid "forward" positioning; use CBG-rich wording instead when
  product labels and COAs support it.

## Medical Claim Guardrails

Do not say or imply that a product diagnoses, treats, cures, prevents, or
manages any disease, illness, symptom, or medical condition.

Avoid claims involving:

- Anxiety.
- Depression.
- Pain.
- Inflammation.
- Sleep disorder.
- Insomnia.
- PTSD.
- Seizures.
- Cancer.
- Arthritis.
- ADHD.
- Stress treatment.
- Cure.
- Heal.
- Medicine.
- Therapy.
- Therapeutic dose.
- Anti-inflammatory.
- Doctor recommended.
- Clinically proven, unless real proof is provided and reviewed later.

Use general wellness language only.

## Tone Guidelines

- Warm, honest, and clear.
- Small-farm and handmade, without sounding unprofessional.
- Adult and wellness-oriented.
- Transparent about placeholders, lab results, batch numbers, and ingredients.
- Plain-English product education.
- No medical promises.
- No recreational or smoke-shop energy.
- No copy that could appeal to children.

## Standard Compliance Copy

Short footer:

The Funni Farm products are non-intoxicating hemp/CBG wellness products intended
for adults. These products are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent
any disease. Please review product details, ingredients, and lab results before
use.

Product-page version:

This is a non-intoxicating hemp/CBG wellness product intended for adults. Review
the product label, ingredients, batch number, and COA before use. Not intended to
diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.

Checkout confirmations:

- I confirm I am of legal age to purchase hemp products.
- I understand these products are non-intoxicating hemp/CBG products.
- I understand these products are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or
  prevent any disease.
- I agree to follow all applicable laws in my location.
